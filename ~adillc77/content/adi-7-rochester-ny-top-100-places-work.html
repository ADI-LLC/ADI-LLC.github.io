ADI is #7 in 2010 Rochester Top 100

Rochester, NY,  November 8, 2010 –   Each year, the Rochester Top 100 recognizes the fastest growing, privately held companies in the area.  This is the first time ADI has appeared on the list, and we have been ranked #7!  This is great news and a testament to our growth and accomplishments, and we congratulate all of our people for their hard work in helping us to achieve this honor.
